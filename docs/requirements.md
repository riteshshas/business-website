# Project Requirements

## Project Name

MS Insurance & Documentation

---

## Goal

Build a modern, trustworthy, responsive business website that helps customers understand available services and contact the business easily.

---

## Primary Goals

- Build trust.
- Reduce customer confusion.
- Explain documentation requirements.
- Increase inquiries.
- Enable online appointment requests.
- Allow customers to upload required documents.
- Provide service information clearly.

---

## Target Audience

- Pune
- Mumbai
- Families
- Working professionals
- Small business owners
- People needing insurance
- People needing documentation assistance
